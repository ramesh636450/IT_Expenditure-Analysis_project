- Business areas
- Countries
- Cost elements
- IT service areas

------------------------------------------------------------

- Significant deviations between actual and planned budgets
- Certain business areas contributing to higher IT costs
- Regional differences in IT spending patterns
- Specific cost elements causing budget overruns

------------------------------------------------------------
